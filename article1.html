Global Leaders Summit 2024: Key Highlights

Date: March 15-17, 2024

The Global Leaders Summit held this year in Geneva brought together heads of state, policymakers, and experts to discuss pressing global issues such as climate change, economic recovery, and international cooperation. Key agreements were reached to foster sustainable development and peace.

Key Outcomes

  • Commitment to reduce carbon emissions by 40% by 2030.
  • New trade agreements fostering economic growth.
  • Initiatives to address global health crises.
